Site Plan

Site Name

Example: The Adventurer’s Guide to Dungeons & Dragons

This name reflects the purpose of the website: a guide for new and experienced D&D players to learn the basics of the game, explore official resources, and find ways to start playing. It’s approachable and connects directly to the official brand of Dungeons & Dragons.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a beginner-friendly introduction to Dungeons & Dragons, while also providing links and summaries of official resources. Visitors will be able to learn what D&D is, understand how to create a character, explore the main rulebooks, and find communities where they can start playing.

Scenarios

  • What rulebooks do I need to start playing Dungeons & Dragons?
  • How do I create my first character step by step?
  • Where can I find official adventures and campaigns to play with my group?

Color Schema

Selected colors and their usage:

  • #2C3E50 (Dark Blue-Gray) – main site background and navigation bar.
  • #DF0000 (D&D Red) – accent color for headers, buttons, and important highlights.
  • #ECF0F1 (Light Gray) – background for content sections for readability.
  • #000000 (Black) – footer and strong contrast text areas.

Typography

  • Cinzel Decorative – used for the main site title and headers to give a medieval/fantasy feel.
  • Inter – used for body text and navigation for clean readability.
